<p><p>The anti-submarine rocket market comprises primarily two types: two-stage and three-stage systems. Two-stage anti-submarine rockets utilize a simpler, more cost-effective design with a primary boost phase followed by a guidance phase, making them suitable for close-range detection and engagement. In contrast, three-stage rockets feature an additional stage, enhancing range, speed, and accuracy, allowing for longer-distance targeting and better performance against advanced submarines. These distinctions cater to varying operational requirements and budget constraints within naval defense strategies.</p></p>
