<p><p>The commercial laundry machine market is primarily segmented into two types: tunnel washers and washer extractors. Tunnel washers are high-capacity machines designed for continuous washing, often used in large operations, where garments pass through a series of stages for efficient processing. In contrast, washer extractors are versatile, standalone machines that combine washing and spinning in one unit, suitable for medium to large laundries. Both types cater to specific needs in commercial settings, ensuring effective and efficient laundry solutions.</p></p>
