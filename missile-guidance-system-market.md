<p><p>The missile guidance system market encompasses several key types. Command guidance systems rely on external commands for navigation. Homing guidance systems autonomously track and intercept targets using onboard sensors. Beam rider systems follow a radar beam emitted from a ground or airborne source. Inertial guidance systems utilize internal sensors to calculate position and direction without external input, while GPS-guided systems leverage satellite signals for precise navigation. Each type serves distinct operational needs, influencing demand and development in the defense sector.</p></p>
