<p><ul><li>Double Magnet</li><li>Single Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The needle counters market is typically divided into two main types: double magnet and single magnet counters. Double magnet counters utilize two opposing magnets to enhance accuracy and reliability in counting needles, effectively minimizing errors during counting processes. In contrast, single magnet counters rely on a single magnet, making them simpler and more cost-effective but potentially less precise. Both types serve essential roles in industries like healthcare and manufacturing, where precise needle tracking is crucial for safety and inventory management.</p></p>
