<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tube furnace market can be categorized into two primary types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical tube furnaces are designed with a vertical orientation, allowing for efficient heating of samples in a compact space, ideal for processes like chemical vapor deposition. In contrast, horizontal tube furnaces have a horizontal layout, offering ease of access for loading and unloading materials, making them suitable for larger samples and diverse applications, such as sintering and annealing in metallurgy. Each type caters to specific operational needs and space requirements.</p></p>
