<p><strong>The Drinking-water Fountains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>BWC</li><li>POU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drinking-water fountains market comprises two primary types: Bottle Filling Stations (BWC) and Point of Use (POU) systems. BWC units provide convenient access to clean water, allowing users to refill their bottles directly at the fountain. POU systems, on the other hand, connect directly to the existing water supply, offering filtered water dispensed through taps or fountains. Both types serve unique environments, such as schools, offices, and public spaces, emphasizing accessibility and sustainability in providing potable water.</p></p>
