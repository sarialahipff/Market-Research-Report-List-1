<p><ul><li>Dipped Headlight</li><li>High Beam</li><li>Fog Lamps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive xenon lighting market is primarily segmented into three types: dipped headlights, high beams, and fog lamps. Dipped headlights are designed for nighttime driving, providing a focused beam to illuminate the road without blinding oncoming drivers. High beams offer a more intense light for improved visibility on open roads. Fog lamps, positioned lower on vehicles, emit a wide, low-pattern beam to cut through fog, rain, or snow, enhancing visibility in adverse weather conditions. Each type serves distinct safety and visibility purposes.</p></p>
