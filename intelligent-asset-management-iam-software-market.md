<p><p>Intelligent Asset Management (IAM) software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based IAM software is hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, accessibility, and reduced maintenance costs. It enables users to access assets and data from anywhere with an internet connection. On-premises IAM software, on the other hand, is installed locally on a company’s servers, providing greater control over data security and compliance, but requiring more investment in infrastructure and maintenance. Each type caters to different business needs and preferences.</p></p>
