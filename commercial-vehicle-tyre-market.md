<p><ul><li>Radial Tyre</li><li>Bias Tyre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle tyre market primarily features two types: radial and bias tyres. Radial tyres utilize steel belts and offer superior flexibility and fuel efficiency, making them ideal for long-haul transport due to improved grip and lower rolling resistance. Conversely, bias tyres have a crisscross tread pattern that enhances durability and load-carrying capacity, suitable for short-distance and off-road applications. Each type caters to specific operational needs, influencing performance, longevity, and overall vehicle efficiency in commercial use.</p></p>
