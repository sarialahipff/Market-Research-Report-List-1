<p><ul><li>DC-DC</li><li>AC-DC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inverters play a critical role in electric vehicles (EVs) by converting electrical energy between different forms. DC-DC inverters step down or step up direct current (DC) voltage levels, enabling efficient power management within the vehicle's battery system. AC-DC inverters, on the other hand, convert alternating current (AC) from charging stations into DC for storage in the vehicle's batteries. Both types are essential for optimizing performance, efficiency, and compatibility with various charging infrastructures in the EV market.</p></p>
