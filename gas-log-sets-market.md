<p><ul><li>Vented Gas Log Set</li><li>Ventless Gas Log Set</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas log sets market is divided into two main types: vented and ventless gas log sets. Vented gas log sets require a chimney or flue to expel combustion gases, providing a traditional fireplace experience with realistic flames. They are typically used for aesthetic appeal and comfort. In contrast, ventless gas log sets operate without a vent, utilizing oxygen from the room for combustion. This type is more energy efficient and can be placed in various locations, but may require safety considerations due to emissions.</p></p>
