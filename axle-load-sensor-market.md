<p><p>The axle load sensor market includes various types based on installation and functionality. Spring-mounted sensors utilize springs to measure load, offering adaptability in dynamic conditions. Pressure-mounted sensors rely on pressure differences to gauge weight effectively, providing high accuracy in confined spaces. Location-mounted sensors are strategically positioned to monitor load distribution across an axle, enhancing safety. Air-mounted sensors use pneumatic systems for load detection, ideal for vehicles with air suspension, ensuring precise measurements in varying loads and conditions.</p></p>
