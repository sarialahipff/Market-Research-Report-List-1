<p><p>The thyroid hormone test market encompasses three primary types: Thyroid-Stimulating Hormone (TSH), Thyroxine (T4), and Triiodothyronine (T3). TSH tests assess the pituitary gland's function and its regulation of thyroid hormones. T4 tests measure the main hormone produced by the thyroid, indicating overall thyroid function. T3 tests evaluate the active hormone responsible for regulating metabolism and energy levels. These tests help diagnose thyroid disorders, guiding treatment decisions and monitoring therapeutic efficacy in patients.</p></p>
