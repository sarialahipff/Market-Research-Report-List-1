<p><p>The ORP (Oxidation-Reduction Potential) sensor market comprises three main types: Glass Type Sensors, ISFET Sensors, and Others. Glass Type Sensors utilize a glass membrane to gauge the electrical potential of a solution, offering high precision. ISFET Sensors (Ion-Sensitive Field-Effect Transistors) utilize semiconductor technology for enhanced durability and miniaturization, suitable for various applications. The "Others" category includes alternative technologies like optical sensors and composite materials that cater to specific industrial needs, driving innovation in water quality monitoring.</p></p>
