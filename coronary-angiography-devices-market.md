<p><p>The coronary angiography devices market comprises various modalities, primarily including X-ray angiography, CT angiography, MR angiography, and others. X-ray angiography is the traditional gold standard for detailed vessel imaging, utilizing contrast agents. CT angiography offers rapid imaging of coronary arteries with advanced detection capabilities. MR angiography employs magnetic resonance technology to visualize blood vessels without radiation exposure. Other methods may include optical coherence tomography and intravascular ultrasound, providing additional diagnostic insights and aiding in treatment planning for cardiovascular diseases.</p></p>
