Distributed Control Systems (DCS) manage complex processes, providing centralized control over production. Programmable Logic Controllers (PLC) automate machinery and streamline manufacturing tasks. Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) systems enable real-time monitoring and control of industrial processes, facilitating remote management. Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) optimize production processes, tracking and documenting the transformation of raw materials into finished goods, ensuring quality and compliance in life sciences environments.</p></p>
