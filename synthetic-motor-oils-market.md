<p><strong>The Synthetic Motor Oils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-Synthetic Motor Oil</li><li>Full Synthetic Motor Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic motor oils market consists of two primary types: semi-synthetic and full synthetic motor oils. Semi-synthetic motor oil blends conventional oil with synthetic base oils, offering improved performance and protection at a lower cost than full synthetics. Full synthetic motor oil, made entirely from synthetic compounds, provides superior performance under extreme conditions, enhanced oxidation stability, and better wear protection. Both types cater to varying consumer needs, with full synthetic oils generally preferred for high-performance and longer-lasting applications.</p></p>
