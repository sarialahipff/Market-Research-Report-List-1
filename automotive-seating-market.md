<p><ul><li>Split Seat</li><li>Bench Seat</li><li>Split Bench Seat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive seating market can be categorized into three main types: split seats, bench seats, and split bench seats. Split seats consist of individual seat units that provide flexibility in configuration, often enhancing comfort and accessibility. Bench seats offer a continuous seating surface, accommodating multiple passengers and maximizing space in smaller vehicles. Split bench seats combine elements of both, featuring a two-part design that allows for added versatility, such as folding down one side to transport larger items while still seating passengers.</p></p>
