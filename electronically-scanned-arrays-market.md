<p><strong>The Electronically Scanned Arrays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronically Scanned Arrays (ESAs) are categorized into active and passive markets based on their technology and functionality. Active ESAs contain transmitter and receiver modules for each element, enabling dynamic beam steering and detailed signal processing, making them suitable for advanced applications like radar and communication. In contrast, passive ESAs lack these transmitting capabilities; they primarily receive signals and rely on external sources for transmission. This distinction influences their use across sectors like defense, aviation, and telecommunications.</p></p>
