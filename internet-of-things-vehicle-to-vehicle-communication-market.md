<p><p>The Internet of Things (IoT) Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) communication market can be categorized into three types: Tethered, Embedded, and Integrated. Tethered communication relies on external devices connecting to vehicles, often through smartphones or other gadgets. Embedded systems involve hardware permanently installed in vehicles, enabling direct communication without external devices. Integrated solutions combine V2V communication with vehicle systems, allowing seamless interaction with other technologies, such as telematics and navigation, enhancing safety and efficiency in transportation networks.</p></p>
